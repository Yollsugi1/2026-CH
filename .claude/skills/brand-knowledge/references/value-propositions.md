## How to Pitch Value (By Situation)

### Initial Sales Call

**Opening:**
"We're not a typical agency. We build owned media engines that drive business results. Let me show you what that means..."

**Show, Don't Tell:**
- Pull up Kuksundang, Inhaze, or Pelicana case based on their category
- Walk through: Challenge → Our Solution → Results
- Connect to their specific situation

**Close:**
"Based on what you've shared, I think [specific framework] would work well for you. Can we schedule a deeper dive?"

### Proposal Presentation

**Structure:**
1. **Your Challenge** (show we understand their pain)
2. **Why Traditional Approaches Fail** (magazine model, generic content, etc.)
3. **Our Solution** (specific framework for them)
4. **Proof It Works** (relevant case study)
5. **What You Get** (deliverables, timeline, investment)
6. **Expected Outcomes** (realistic goals)

**Emphasis:**
- Business outcomes over pretty pictures
- Strategic thinking over execution
- Long-term asset over short-term campaign

### Handling Objections

**"Your price seems high"**
→ "Let's talk about cost vs. investment. If we drive [X sales/awareness], what's that worth? Plus, we have performance options that reduce your risk."

**"We've tried agencies before, didn't work"**
→ "What didn't work specifically? [Listen] That's exactly why we do [different approach]. Let me show you..."

**"We need results fast"**
→ "Fast depends on what we're measuring. Engagement? We can move that in 30 days. Sales? Need 60-90 days for attribution data. Let's set realistic milestones."

**"Can't we just hire someone in-house?"**
→ "You could hire one person for ₩4M+/month. You get their specific skills. With us, you get strategist + producer + designer + writer, proven frameworks, and no employment risk. Plus we start immediately."

**"How do I know you'll deliver?"**
→ "Let's do a 3-month pilot. Clear KPIs, monthly reviews, easy exit if not working. Sound fair?"

## Pricing Value Communication

### Don't Lead With Price

**Wrong approach:**
"Our packages start at ₩2.5M/month..."

**Right approach:**
"Let me understand what you're trying to achieve... [discuss needs] ...based on that, here's what I recommend and why... [show value] ...and here's the investment."

### Frame Investment, Not Cost

**Cost framing:**
"₩3M per month for content"

**Investment framing:**
"₩3M per month to build an owned media engine that drives [specific outcome]. Here's the ROI calculation..."

### Offer Options, Not Single Price

**Single price:**
"Our standard package is ₩4M/month"

**Options:**
"Based on your goals, here are three approaches:
- Option A: [scope] at ₩2.5M
- Option B: [scope] at ₩3.5M
- Option C: [scope] at performance-based"

Most clients pick middle option, but having choice creates agency.
