# Proposal Templates

Reusable proposal structures for different client types and situations. Adapt sections based on specific needs.

---

## Universal Proposal Structure

Every proposal should include these core sections, adapted to context:

1. **Executive Summary** (1 page)
2. **Understanding Your Challenge** (1-2 pages)
3. **Our Approach** (2-3 pages)
4. **What You'll Get** (1-2 pages)
5. **Why Creatorhood** (1 page)
6. **Investment & Timeline** (1 page)
7. **Next Steps** (1 page)

## Quick Proposal Builder

**For rapid proposal creation, use this checklist:**

□ Cover page with client logo and compelling title
□ Executive summary (1 page max)
□ Show you understand their challenge (don't skip this!)
□ Present ONE clear strategic approach (not multiple options)
□ Specific deliverables and timeline
□ Relevant case study (match their situation)
□ Clear investment and what's included/excluded
□ Next steps (make it easy to say yes)

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**

❌ Too long (keep under 10 pages)
❌ Too much about us, not enough about them
❌ Vague deliverables ("content creation" vs. "15 pieces: 5 videos, 10 images")
❌ No proof (always include relevant case study)
❌ Confusing pricing (be crystal clear)
❌ No clear next steps

---

**Remember:** Proposal should be conversation starter, not contract. Leave room for discussion and refinement. Make it easy for client to say yes by removing friction and uncertainty.
