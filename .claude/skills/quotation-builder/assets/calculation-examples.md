## Example 1: F&B Brand - Full Service

### Client Context
- **Industry:** Korean restaurant chain
- **Size:** 15 locations, ₩2B annual revenue
- **Current state:** 8K Instagram followers, inconsistent posting
- **Goal:** Expand to younger demographics, build content presence
- **Budget:** ₩3-5M/month

### Scope Discussion
Client wants:
- Regular content (2-3x per week)
- Professional quality
- Instagram focus (where their customers are)
- Help with overall strategy

### Internal Cost Calculation

**Team Time Needed:**
```
Strategy Director: 15 hours/month × ₩120K = ₩1,800K
Content Producer: 35 hours/month × ₩80K = ₩2,800K
Designer: 15 hours/month × ₩70K = ₩1,050K
Social Manager: 20 hours/month × ₩70K = ₩1,400K
---
Total team time: ₩7,050K

Target margin: 50%
Minimum price to be profitable: ₩3,525K
```

**Deliverables to Match Budget:**
At ₩3.5M, we can sustainably deliver:
- 5 videos/month (₩400K each standard tier = ₩2M value)
- 10 images/month (₩150K each = ₩1.5M value)
- Copywriting (₩500K value)
- Social management (₩1M value)
- Strategy (₩500K value)
---
Total perceived value: ₩5.5M
Actual price: ₩3.5M (client feels they're getting deal)

### Final Quotation

**OPTION A: Premium Partnership** (Recommended)
- **Price:** ₩4,000,000/month
- **Includes:**
  - 5 videos (premium tier, film look)
  - 12 images (additional 2)
  - Multi-platform (Instagram + YouTube Shorts)
  - Weekly strategy check-ins
- **Why:** If they want highest quality and broader reach

**OPTION B: Core Partnership**
- **Price:** ₩3,500,000/month ← Most will choose this
- **Includes:**
  - 5 videos (standard tier)
  - 10 images
  - Instagram management
  - Bi-weekly strategy sessions
- **Why:** Perfect fit for goals and budget

**OPTION C: Starter Partnership**
- **Price:** ₩2,500,000/month
- **Includes:**
  - 3 videos
  - 8 images
  - Instagram posting only (no active management)
  - Monthly check-ins
- **Why:** If budget is tighter than expected

### Pricing Justification for Client

"Option B at ₩3.5M per month delivers 15 pieces of premium content and full Instagram management. Compared to:
- Hiring in-house: ₩5-6M/month (salary + benefits for 1-2 people)
- Traditional agency: ₩5-8M/month for similar scope
- Freelancers: ₩2-3M but inconsistent and no strategy

You get proven frameworks, team capability, and sustainable quality at a competitive price."

### 6-Month Projection

**Month 1 (Foundation):** ₩4,500,000
- Intensive strategy work
- Brand positioning
- First content batch

**Months 2-6:** ₩3,500,000 × 5 = ₩17,500,000

**Total 6 months:** ₩22,000,000

**Expected ROI for client:**
- Engaged younger demographic (measurable in analytics)
- Consistent professional content (vs. current sporadic posts)
- Brand repositioning complete
- Organic reach growth (no paid ad dependency)

## Example 2: E-commerce Brand - Performance Partnership

### Client Context
- **Industry:** Korean skincare e-commerce
- **Size:** ₩200M annual GMV, 80% online
- **Current state:** Paid ads working but expensive (₩15M/month spend)
- **Goal:** Reduce CAC, prove organic content drives sales
- **Budget:** "Show us results first"

### Client's Current Economics

```
Monthly paid ad spend: ₩15,000,000
Monthly sales from ads: ₩45,000,000
Current CAC: ₩25,000 per customer
ROAS: 3.0x (₩3 revenue per ₩1 spent)

They want:
- Better ROAS (3.5-4x)
- Lower CAC (₩15-18K range)
- Proof that organic content works
```

### Performance Model Calculation

**Option A: Pure Performance**
```
Base fee: ₩0
Revenue share: 18% of attributed sales

Scenario 1 (Conservative):
- ₩8M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩1.44M
- Client's effective CAC: ₩18K (if 80 customers)
- ROAS for client: Can't calculate (no ad spend, organic)
- Client wins: Lower cost than paid ads

Scenario 2 (Target):
- ₩15M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩2.7M
- Client wins: ₩15M in sales for ₩2.7M cost = 5.6x ROAS

Scenario 3 (Stretch):
- ₩25M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩4.5M
- Client wins: ₩25M in sales for ₩4.5M cost = 5.6x ROAS

Our risk: If we generate ₩0, we earn ₩0
```

**Option B: Hybrid** (Recommended)
```
Base fee: ₩800,000/month
Revenue share: 12% of attributed sales

Why hybrid:
- Base covers minimum production costs
- Lower percentage makes it sustainable
- Still mostly performance-driven

Scenario 1 (Conservative):
- ₩8M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩960K = ₩1.76M
- Better for us than pure performance
- Still great for client (4.5x ROAS)

Scenario 2 (Target):
- ₩15M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩1.8M = ₩2.6M
- Client gets 5.8x ROAS
- We earn sustainable amount

Scenario 3 (Stretch):
- ₩25M monthly attributed sales
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩3M = ₩3.8M
- Client gets 6.6x ROAS
- We're well-compensated for performance
```

### Final Quotation

**OPTION A: Pure Performance** (Highest Client Upside)
- Base: ₩0
- Share: 18% of attributed sales
- Client risk: Zero if no sales
- Our risk: High

**OPTION B: Hybrid** (Recommended - Balanced)
- Base: ₩800,000/month
- Share: 12% of attributed sales
- Client risk: Minimal base, mostly performance
- Our risk: Moderate, sustainable

**OPTION C: Tiered** (Scales with Success)
- Base: ₩500,000/month
- Share: 0-₩5M = 20%, ₩5-10M = 15%, ₩10M+ = 12%
- Encourages growth
- Benefits both as volume increases

### Attribution Setup

**Required tracking:**
- UTM parameters: utm_source=creatorhood&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=[campaign]
- Coupon codes: CHMAG10, CREATOR15, SKINCARE20
- GA4 e-commerce events
- Monthly reconciliation (transparent sheet)

**Content strategy:**
- 10-12 pieces/month (Reels, Stories, Feed)
- 100% conversion-focused (problem→solution, before/after, tutorials, social proof)
- A/B test hooks and CTAs weekly
- Double down on what converts

### 3-Month Pilot Projection

**Month 1:** Setup + testing
- Attributed sales: ₩4-6M (ramping up)
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩600K = ₩1.4M

**Month 2:** Optimization
- Attributed sales: ₩8-10M (learning what works)
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩1.08M = ₩1.88M

**Month 3:** Scaling
- Attributed sales: ₩12-15M (consistent system)
- Our earnings: ₩800K + ₩1.62M = ₩2.42M

**3-month total:**
- Client's sales: ₩27M
- Client's cost: ₩5.7M
- Client's ROAS: 4.7x
- Our earnings: ₩5.7M over 3 months

**Decision point at Month 3:**
- If working: Extend and scale
- If not: Analyze why, adjust or exit

## Example 3: Heritage Brand - Repositioning Project

### Client Context
- **Industry:** Korean chicken chain (26 years old)
- **Size:** 200+ locations, well-known but "old" perception
- **Current state:** 25K Instagram, generic content, losing to trendy competitors
- **Goal:** Reposition as cool/retro, attract 20-30s demographic
- **Budget:** "This is strategic, budget flexible"

### Scope Discussion

This is transformation, not just content:
- Brand repositioning strategy
- New visual identity (retro system)
- Content series development
- 6-month execution

### Project Calculation

**What's required:**

```
STRATEGY WORK (Month 1-2):
Strategy Director: 60 hours × ₩120K = ₩7.2M
Designer: 50 hours × ₩80K = ₩4M
Research & workshops: ₩1M
---
Subtotal strategy: ₩12.2M

VISUAL IDENTITY (Month 1-2):
Retro visual system development
Photography style guide
Film look cinematography standards
Template system
---
Value: ₩3-4M

ONGOING CONTENT (Months 2-6):
5 months × ₩4M/month = ₩20M
(Premium production, film look, 20 pieces/month)

TOTAL PROJECT VALUE: ₩35-36M
```

### Pricing Strategy

**What we could charge:**
- If priced as pure project: ₩35M
- But that's a big number upfront
- Better: Phase it for easier approval

**What we'll propose:**

**PHASE 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)** - ₩8,000,000
- Heritage audit and positioning workshop
- Retro visual system development
- Content series concepts (3 series)
- First 10 pieces of content

**PHASE 2: Launch & Momentum (Month 3-4)** - ₩4,500,000/month
- 3 content series running (20 pieces/month)
- Film look premium production
- Full Instagram management
- Bi-weekly strategy sessions

**PHASE 3: Optimization (Month 5-6)** - ₩4,000,000/month
- Continued content production
- Performance optimization
- Scaling successful elements
- Monthly strategy reviews

**Total 6-Month Investment:** ₩25,000,000

**Compared to:**
- Traditional agency rebrand: ₩30-50M (strategy only, no execution)
- In-house team: ₩30M+ over 6 months (salary, tools, ramp-up time)
- Piecemeal freelancers: ₩20-25M (but no strategic cohesion)

### Final Quotation

**Single Option (Strategic Project)**

This is transformation work, not just "social media management," so we present it as comprehensive program:

**6-MONTH REPOSITIONING PROGRAM**
- **Total Investment:** ₩25,000,000
- **Payment Schedule:**
  - Phase 1 (Month 1-2): ₩8M
  - Phase 2 (Month 3-4): ₩9M (₩4.5M × 2)
  - Phase 3 (Month 5-6): ₩8M (₩4M × 2)

**What Makes This Strategic:**
- Not just content, but repositioning
- Proven playbook (reference Pelicana case if we had it)
- Creates long-term asset (visual identity, content series)
- Measurable outcome (sentiment shift, demographic growth)

**What They Get:**
- Heritage repositioned as strength
- Distinctive retro visual identity
- 3 signature content series
- 100+ pieces of premium content
- Brand transformation documentation

**Expected ROI:**
- Brand perception shift (measurable in sentiment)
- Younger demographic growth (Instagram analytics)
- Competitive differentiation (unique visual identity)
- Media coverage potential (interesting story)
- Long-term value (not just campaign, but new brand positioning)

## Example 4: Startup - Starter Package

### Client Context
- **Industry:** Sports tech app
- **Stage:** Pre-seed, just launched
- **Size:** 2 founders, no revenue yet
- **Current state:** 500 Instagram followers, founder-posted content
- **Goal:** Build brand presence, attract users and investors
- **Budget:** <₩2M/month

### Budget Reality Check

```
What they need:
- Professional content
- Consistent posting
- Brand development
- Help with strategy

What they can afford:
- ₩1.5-2M/month max

Can we be profitable at ₩2M?

Team time available at ₩2M (with 50% margin):
Total time budget: ₩1M

Producer: 15 hours × ₩80K = ₩1.2M (over budget!)
Need to be more efficient...

Realistic at ₩2M:
Producer: 12 hours (1.5 days) × ₩80K = ₩960K
Designer: 5 hours × ₩70K = ₩350K
---
Total: ₩1.31M (leaves some buffer)

This limits us to:
- 3 videos/month (simple tier)
- 7 images/month
- Basic strategy only
- Limited revisions
```

### Scope Matching Budget

**What we CAN do at ₩2M:**
- 10 pieces of content/month (3 video, 7 image)
- Instagram posting
- Basic strategy
- Bi-weekly check-ins

**What we CAN'T do:**
- Full social management
- Community management
- Premium production
- Multi-platform

### Final Quotation

**STARTER PACKAGE**
- **Price:** ₩2,000,000/month
- **Commitment:** 3 months minimum
- **Includes:**
  - Brand positioning workshop
  - 3 simple videos/month
  - 7 image posts/month
  - Instagram posting (scheduled)
  - Bi-weekly strategy calls (30 min)
  - Monthly performance report

**Upgrade Path Defined:**
After 3 months (or when funding raised):
- Growth Package: ₩3.5M/month (15 pieces, full management)
- Full Service: ₩4-5M/month (20+ pieces, multi-platform)

### Pricing Justification

"We understand you're early stage. This Starter Package is designed to give you professional presence without breaking the bank. At ₩2M/month:

- You get 10 professional pieces (vs. founder-created)
- Consistent posting (vs. sporadic)
- Strategic guidance (vs. guessing)
- Room to grow with you (when you raise, we scale up)

Compare to:
- Hiring: Can't afford quality full-time at this stage
- Freelancer: ₩1-1.5M but no strategy or consistency
- DIY: Free but doesn't look professional to investors

Think of it as: ₩66K per day for professional brand presence during your critical early growth phase."

### 3-Month Projection

**Month 1:** Foundation
- Workshop and setup
- First 10 pieces
- Instagram optimized

**Months 2-3:** Momentum
- 10 pieces per month
- Growing follower base
- Content for investor pitches

**Total investment:** ₩6,000,000 for 3 months
**After 3 months:** Re-evaluate based on:
- Did you raise funding? (upgrade to full service)
- Are you generating revenue? (upgrade to growth package)
- Still bootstrapping? (continue starter or performance model)

## Calculation Principles (Summary)

### 1. Know Your Costs

```
Team Time × Hourly Rate = Base Cost
Base Cost ÷ (1 - Target Margin) = Minimum Price

Example:
₩2M team time ÷ (1 - 0.5) = ₩4M minimum price for 50% margin
```

### 2. Match Value to Client

**Small Business / Startup:** ₩1.5-3M/month
- Limited budget
- Simple needs
- Growth potential

**Mid-Size Business:** ₩3-5M/month
- Reasonable budget
- Comprehensive needs
- Proven business

**Enterprise:** ₩5-10M+/month
- Substantial budget
- Complex needs
- High expectations

### 3. Provide Options

**High (Anchor):** 120-150% of target
**Target (Recommended):** 100% of what you want
**Low (Alternative):** 70-80% of target (or different model)

Most will choose target (as intended).

### 4. Justify with Comparisons

Always compare to alternatives:
- In-house hiring costs
- Traditional agency rates
- Freelancer inconsistency
- DIY opportunity cost

### 5. Performance Models When Applicable

For e-commerce:
- Pure performance: 15-20% revenue share
- Hybrid: ₩500K-1M base + 10-15% share
- Tiered: Decreasing % as volume increases

For others:
- Fixed monthly retainer
- Project-based with milestones
- Starter packages for testing

---

**Remember:** Every calculation should balance:
1. **Our profitability** (sustainable margins)
2. **Client value** (worth it for them)
3. **Market competitiveness** (not wildly out of range)
4. **Strategic positioning** (premium but accessible)

Use these examples as templates, but always calculate based on actual scope, client context, and your team's capacity.
