## Video Production Services

### Shortform Video (Reels, Shorts, TikTok)

**SIMPLE TIER**
- **Price:** ₩200,000-300,000 per video
- **Scope:**
  - Single location shoot (2-3 hours)
  - Basic equipment (smartphone or DSLR)
  - Simple editing (cuts, transitions, text)
  - Licensed music
  - 1 round of revisions
- **Timeline:** 5-7 days from concept to delivery
- **Best for:** Regular content, volume production
- **Examples:** Product features, simple tutorials, day-in-life

**STANDARD TIER**
- **Price:** ₩300,000-500,000 per video
- **Scope:**
  - Multi-location or controlled studio shoot (half-day)
  - Professional equipment (cinema camera, lighting, audio)
  - Advanced editing (color grading, motion graphics)
  - Original or licensed music
  - 2 rounds of revisions
- **Timeline:** 7-10 days from concept to delivery
- **Best for:** Brand content, campaign pieces
- **Examples:** Product launches, brand stories, customer testimonials

**PREMIUM TIER**
- **Price:** ₩500,000-800,000 per video
- **Scope:**
  - Full-day shoot with art direction
  - Cinema-grade equipment (film look, professional lighting)
  - Documentary or narrative approach
  - Extensive post (color grading, sound design, graphics)
  - Film-look treatment or special aesthetics
  - 3 rounds of revisions
- **Timeline:** 10-14 days from concept to delivery
- **Best for:** Signature content, hero pieces, campaigns
- **Examples:** Documentary series (Kuksundang style), brand films (Pelicana retro), cultural stories
